osudroid!bench

Description

This is an osu!droid-related benchmarking project, where you can test your phone to play a provided osu! beatmap that is overmapped.

Requirements

This web project only requires the osu!droid app, and the osu! beatmap which can be downloaded by clicking here

Instructions

Import the beatmap, and play it on auto. Make sure the FPS setting is enabled and have an eye on it.

Table

Here is a table. You can look around and check the performance of each phone provided. It could whether be useful or not useful, but it would depend on your experience. If you want your phone's benchmark score to show up, you can click here to fill this form
